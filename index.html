Crown Finish Caves

Crown Finish Caves is a cheese aging facility located in a repurposed 1850's "lagering tunnel" thirty feet below the street in Crown Heights Brooklyn.

We are working primarily with Parish Hill Creamery in Vermont starting in May of 2014. Currently we are distributing the newly available Parish Hill Creamery cheeses in the NYC metropolitan area.
